How do dental implants work, and are they available in Karachi?

+

Yes — dental implants in Karachi are available at Dental Vision 2000. Dr. Furqan is a specialist implantologist who has placed hundreds of successful implants. A dental implant is a titanium post inserted into the jawbone to replace a missing tooth root. A crown is then attached on top, creating a replacement tooth that looks, feels, and functions exactly like a natural tooth. Implants are permanent, preserve jawbone health, and are considered the gold standard in modern dentistry. Contact us to find out if you are a suitable candidate.
